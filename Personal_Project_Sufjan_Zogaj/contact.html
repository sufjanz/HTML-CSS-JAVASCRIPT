Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is at 3:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out are available upon request and subject to availability.

Do you offer airport transportation?

Yes, we provide luxury airport transfer service for $75 each way. Please contact our concierge to arrange transportation.

Is parking available?

We offer complimentary valet parking for all hotel guests. Self-parking is also available in our secure underground garage.

Are pets allowed?

Yes, we welcome pets with a $50 per night fee. We provide pet amenities including beds, bowls, and treats.

What dining options are available?

Our award-winning restaurant serves dinner from 6:00 PM to 11:00 PM. We also offer 24/7 room service with an extensive menu.

Do you have meeting facilities?

Yes, we have 5 fully equipped meeting rooms and conference facilities with state-of-the-art technology and catering services.
