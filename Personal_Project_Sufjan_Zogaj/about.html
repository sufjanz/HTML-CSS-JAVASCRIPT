Our Story

A legacy of luxury hospitality since 1985

Welcome to Premium Hotel, where luxury meets comfort in the heart of the city. Since 1985, we have been providing exceptional hospitality services to discerning travelers from around the world.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every aspect of your stay, from our elegantly appointed rooms to our world-class amenities. Whether you're here for business or leisure, our dedicated staff ensures that every moment of your visit exceeds your expectations.

Experience the perfect blend of modern sophistication and timeless elegance at Premium Hotel, where every guest is treated like royalty.

Our Journey

Milestones in our pursuit of excellence

1985

Grand Opening

Premium Hotel opened its doors with 50 luxury rooms, setting new standards for hospitality in the city.

1992

First Expansion

Added the luxury spa and fitness center, becoming the first hotel in the district to offer comprehensive wellness facilities.

1998

Award Recognition

Received our first "Hotel of the Year" award from the International Hospitality Association.

2005

Major Renovation

Complete renovation of all rooms and public areas, introducing modern technology while preserving classic elegance.

2015

Rooftop Addition

Opened the iconic rooftop infinity pool and sky bar, offering breathtaking city views.

2024

Sustainability Initiative

Launched our comprehensive green program, achieving carbon neutrality and LEED Gold certification.
